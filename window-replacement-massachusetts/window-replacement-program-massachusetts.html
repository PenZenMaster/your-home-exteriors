Window Replacement Program Massachusetts

Windows are an integral part of any home, providing natural light and ventilation. Unfortunately, windows can become damaged over time due to age, storms, or other damage. As a result, it is important for Massachusetts homeowners to be aware of the available window replacement programs in the state.

The Massachusetts Window Replacement Program (MWRP) offers grants to eligible residents for replacing their old and outdated windows with energy efficient models. This program helps reduce energy costs by improving insulation and reducing air leakage - both of which can help keep homes more comfortable during hot or cold weather! Additionally, the MWRP provides a tax credit for up to 50% of the cost of purchasing qualifying windows.

In order to qualify for this program, homeowners must meet certain criteria such as having owned and lived in their home for at least one year prior to applying and having a household income that does not exceed certain levels. Furthermore, applicants must also provide proof that they have purchased ENERGY STAR rated windows from an approved vendor. Once all requirements are met, the homeowner may apply online or by mail for their grant along with supporting documentation like receipts showing purchase prices of qualifying materials.

The MWRP is an excellent opportunity for Massachusetts homeowners looking to upgrade their inefficient windows while saving money on energy costs! Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Window Replacement Program Massachusetts?
The Window Replacement Program Massachusetts is a program that provides financial assistance to homeowners in the form of grants and low-interest loans to help them replace their existing windows with energy efficient models.
Who is eligible for this window replacement program?
Homeowners who meet certain income requirements are eligible for the Window Replacement Program Massachusetts. Eligible applicants must have an annual gross household income at or below 80 percent of the median income for their area as determined by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD).
Are there any requirements to participate in this program?
Yes, applicants must be current on all property taxes and mortgages, and they must also own and occupy the home where new windows will be installed. Additionally, if an applicant’s home currently has storm windows, they must agree to remove them prior to installing new windows under this program.
How much funding is available through this window replacement program?
Funding amounts vary depending on each individual application; however, eligible homeowners may receive up to $10,000 in grants or up to $20,000 in 0% interest loans through this program.
When can I apply for this window replacement program?
Applications are accepted year-round; however funds are limited and distributed on a first come, first served basis until exhausted annually.
