Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Window Replacement Program Massachusetts?
The Window Replacement Program Massachusetts is a program designed to help homeowners replace inefficient windows with energy-efficient ones, in order to save on energy costs.
What are the eligibility requirements for the program?
Eligibility requirements vary depending on funding sources; however, generally speaking, applicants must be residential homeowners and their income must be within certain limits.
How much does window replacement cost through this program?
The exact cost of window replacement depends on the size, type, and number of windows being replaced. However, this program typically provides financial assistance to cover up to 50% of eligible costs.
Are there any tax credits available for window replacement?
Yes; eligible homeowners may be able to receive a federal tax credit that covers up to 10% of the total cost of qualified energy-efficient windows, up to $200 per window.
Where can I find more information about the Window Replacement Program Massachusetts?
For more information about this program, please visit www.mass.gov/windowreplacementprogram
